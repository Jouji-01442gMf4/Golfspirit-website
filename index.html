Golfspirit — Golf for everyone in Amsterdam-Zuidoost

OGAR Golfspirit is a nonprofit committed to removing barriers so people of all ages and abilities can enjoy golf. Outdoor lessons, accessible facilities, and community programs.

Gallery

About Golf

Golf is a low-impact, inclusive sport suitable for beginners, children, seniors, and people with limited mobility. It promotes balance, coordination, concentration and social connection. Golfspirit runs adapted lessons, community meetups and introductory trial sessions so new members can experience the sport before committing.

  • Free introductory lesson(s) for new students
  • Outdoor & indoor options in bad weather

The Offer

Lessons run at fixed times during the week (Wednesday afternoon and Saturday), with flexible scheduling on request. New students can observe and take free trial lessons as part of the introduction program.

Location & Accessibility

We are located near Kraaiennest (Amsterdam-Zuidoost). The site is wheelchair accessible and easy to reach by bike, public transport (Kraaiennest metro) or car. Nearby parks like Nelson Mandela Park provide green space for practice.

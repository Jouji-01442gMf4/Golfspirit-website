Introduction

Ogar Golfspirit is a nonprofit organisation committed to making golf accessible to everyone in Amsterdam Zuid-Oost. Our mission is to remove barriers so that people of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities can enjoy the benefits of the sport. Golf encourages outdoor activity, promotes physical fitness, and supports mental well-being by combining exercise, focus, and relaxation. We aim to create opportunities for people to connect, learn, and grow together through the game.

About Golf

Golf is more than just a sport. It is a lifelong activity that can be adapted to individual abilities, making it inclusive for beginners, children, seniors, and people with limited mobility. Because it is low-impact, golf provides a safe way to stay active without placing excessive strain on the body. Playing regularly helps improve balance, coordination, concentration, and overall health. Beyond the physical benefits, golf is also highly social. It encourages people to meet, share experiences, and build friendships in a welcoming environment. These qualities make golf a valuable activity for communities, where both young and old can participate, support one another, and enjoy meaningful time outdoors.

Golfspirit is located near Kraaiennest in Amsterdam-Zuidoost, an area well connected by public transport with the Kraaiennest metro station just a short walk away. The neighbourhood is surrounded by parks, open green spaces, and recreational facilities, making it a natural setting for outdoor sports and activities. Nearby areas such as Nelson Mandela Park and Bijlmerpark provide additional opportunities for recreation and community events. The location is wheelchair accessible and easy to reach by bike, car, or public transit, ensuring that people of all ages and abilities can join. With its mix of cultural diversity, green surroundings, and strong sense of community, Kraaiennest is the ideal place for making golf more accessible to everyone in Amsterdam-Zuidoost.

The offer

Following golf lessons is possible at two fixed times during the week, namely on Wednesday afternoon and Saturday. In consultation it is possible to follow lessons at different times. In the case of bad weather it is possible to switch to the indoor location. Following free trial lesson(s) is part of the introduction program. New students can always come and watch and have a taste of the programme during an outdoor lesson. The starting point is that new students get a good idea of the content of the golf lessons before a contribution is requested.
